In compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173), the information collected through this Visitor Pre-Registration Form—whether submitted for an individual visitor or on behalf of a group—shall be used solely for security, safety, access control, and visitor management purposes.

The personal data provided, including names, contact details, identification information, and uploaded copies of valid IDs, will be:
•	Collected and processed lawfully and fairly
•	Accessed only by authorized personnel
•	Stored securely and retained for a period of two (2) years from the date of visit, unless a longer retention period is required by law, regulation, or legitimate security and audit purposes. After the retention period, the data shall be securely disposed in accordance with applicable data protection and information security policies.
•	Protected against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or misuse

All personal information collected will not be shared with third parties except when required by law, regulation, or upon lawful order of government authorities.

For group registrations, the submitting representative confirms that they are authorized to provide the personal data of the listed individuals and that all such individuals have been informed of and have consented to the collection, processing, storage, and use of their personal data for the purposes stated above.

Visitors may request the deletion or blocking of their personal data after the purpose for which it was collected has been fulfilled, subject to applicable laws, security requirements, and legitimate interests of the organization. Data required to be retained under law, regulation, or for security, audit, or legal purposes shall be kept for the required retention period before secure disposal.
